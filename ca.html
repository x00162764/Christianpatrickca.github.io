My Project Idea is a Private Network between two fixed locations, a virtual network can securely and privately communicate and have a relationship with another virtual network.

I came up with this Idea by looking at real-life situations where this would be applicable. For Example, two or more garda stations in different locations, can share data and communicate with each other privately. This is important to effectively solve crime and appropriately guard and help the community. Ireland isn't like America where geographically and economically speaking States would need to have valuable and secure ways to network with each other, the Republic of Ireland is a lot smaller.
Even so, Police stations in every county must have continuous access to one other as well as communication to headquarters in the capital, Dublin. After some research, I was amazed to find that networking in Ireland is quite poor and not up to standard. The Police force in Ireland uses the Pulse system to police the country and communicate with each other to collectively solve crime. According to a 2017 article 178 garda stations don’t have access to this system and must result to manually calling each office to relay information. We are now in a day and age where technology is of the utmost importance and not being technologically astute is very dangerous.
A Very Well-executed and managed Private Network is something that should be implemented. Virtual Private Networks are networks that offer encryption and other security measures to secure data when it is being transmitted from one place to another.

There are different types of VPNs, but there are two particular VPNs that I will be focusing on:

1. Remote Acess VPNs
2. Site-to-Site VPNs

Site–to–Site VPNS


Site–to–Site VPNs establish an encrypted link between two separate networks, this is usual in the case of branches or offices in different geographical Locations. Each end has its own independent network with its own set of devices. They will then have a VPN Gateway or router that will encrypt the communication between each end of the VPN when data is sent or received. Authentication and Encryption Protocols are used to securely transmit data, and IPsec is a popular one.
